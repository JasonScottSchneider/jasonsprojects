Self-Paced Reading Task for linguistic experiments of sentence processing: Please click HERE

Built as V10

This version implements questions after sentence stimuli and exports whether participants answered correctly for the questions following sentences. Linguists will find this necessary to ensure that participants are actively engaged during the experiment, a cognitive load is applied to memory, and participants do not form habits of pressing buttons rapidly like knee jerk reactions.
